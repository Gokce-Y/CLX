Deploy a high-performance waste detection model optimized for automated recycling and smart waste management. This project offers a pre-trained computer vision model and an annotated dataset, identifying glass, paper, cardboard, plastic, and metal for environmental cleanup initiatives.

## Ways to Use TrashNet Model

1. **AI-Powered Sorting Bins:** Integrate the model into "smart" disposal stations in offices or malls to guide users in real-time, displaying which bin to use based on the material identified in their hand.
2. **Reverse Vending Machines:** Power automated kiosks that accept and sort recyclable containers like glass bottles and metal cans, providing rewards or deposits back to consumers instantly.
3. **Municipality Contamination Audits:** Deploy the model on waste collection trucks equipped with cameras to identify high levels of "trash" mixed into "paper" or "plastic" recycling loads, helping cities target education efforts.
4. **Autonomous Litter Robots:** Implement the model in small, outdoor cleaning robots designed to roam parks or beaches, allowing them to distinguish between natural debris and actionable recyclables like plastic bottles or metal cans.
5. **Industrial Waste Stream Monitoring:** Use the model at manufacturing facilities to track the volume of different packaging materials—such as cardboard and metal—entering the waste stream to help optimize sustainable procurement and disposal strategies.
